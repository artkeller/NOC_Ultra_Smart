## üîí Security Best Practices
Since this tool runs entirely in your browser (**Serverless/Client-side**):
1. **No Data Collection:** The tool does not send your monitored URLs to any third-party server.
2. **Local Storage:** All configuration is stored in your browser's `localStorage`. Be aware that anyone with physical access to your browser can see your monitored URLs.
3. **External Links:** Be cautious when clicking links within the panel if you have added untrusted URLs.
